# Flash Toolbox 刷机工具箱

*Made by ytdttj , coded in C++.*

致力于简化刷机过程，让更多人享受刷机的乐趣

*Beta 1.0 and Beta 1.1 are tested by MarkFu.*<br>
`I am not responsible for anything might happen to your phone!`

### 目前支持功能
1.去除boot校验<br>
2.安装APP<br>
3.激活冰箱、空调狗、小黑屋黑阈<br>
4.类原生叹号杀手<br>
5.sideload线刷<br>
6.刷入Recovery<br>
7.刷入Boot/System.img<br>
8.（部分机型）解锁Bootloader<br>
